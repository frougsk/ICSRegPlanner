# smiCS
- smiCS is an ICS Registration Planner created by group JELly.ace, students of the University of the Philippines - Los Banos, for compliance in their class CMSC 22.

# How to Use

- Upon launch, you will be asked to give the right credentials in order to log in. If you don't have an account in SMICS, you can create one through "Create User."
- Fill in your information, specify your degree program, and click Create.

- You will then be directed to the Dashboard where you can view information on SMICS: how it works, about the creators, and the references used.

- Clicking the menu button at the top left corner will toggle a sidebar where you can view other features.

- The Course will lead you to a page where you can view the courses of UPLB ICS. Courses can be filtered by degree program, searching the course code, and searching  the course name.

- The Planner will lead you to a page where you can plan your courses for the semester. In the page, you will see a calendar visualizer where your schedule can be plot out, a warning pannel for conflict and success course adds, and your course basket. Below the visualizer is where you can view course offerings and add courses to your basket.

# NOTE: The basket will not add any course that conflicts with other courses or is not available to your program!

- Once satisified with your courses, you may Save your schedule and come back to it anytime. 

- At the top right corner, clicking on your name leads to a profile page where you can view your information. You may either sign out, which will redirect you to the log in page, or you may exit, which closes the program.
